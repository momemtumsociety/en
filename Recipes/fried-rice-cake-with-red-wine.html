Fried Rice Cake with Red Wine (Recommended)

Ingredients

BAIGUO (Rice Cake) 5 pieces

Egg 3 untis

Sauce and Others

Light soy sauce 1.5 tablespoon

Red Wine 1 tablespoon

Instructions

  1. Refrigerated or frozen BAIGUO, if any, needs to be rewarmed.
  2. Then, slice it. Use the knife method of slicing, not for sawing. (If you feel it sticking to the knife, wash the knife with water.)
  3. Once cut, wash the BAIGUO in water and drain.
  4. Beat the eggs and set aside.
  5. Heat a pan, add oil and fry the eggs until half cooked, remove from pan and set aside.
  6. Heat a pan, add the oil and stir-fry the BAIGUO.
  7. Add the Red Wine and Light Soy Sauce and stir-fry. Add a little boiling water and turn up the heat to reduce the sauce.
  8. Finally, add the eggs and toss them to coat well before removing from the pan.
