Spicy Fried Rice Cake

Ingredients

Sliced BAIGUO (Rice Cake) 360g

Chopped garlic 5g

White sesame 20g

Sauce and Others

Light soy sauce 1 tablespoon

Chilli coarse 1 tablespoon

Tomato sauce 1 tablespoon

Sugar 1/2 tablespoon

Korean Spicy Sauce 1 tablespoon

Water 100g

Instructions

  1. Take a bowl and mix chopped garlic, light soy sauce, chilli coarse, tomato sauce, sugar, korean spicy sauce and water together.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the BAIGUO until golden brown on both sides.
  3. Then pour the mixed sauce in, turn down the heat and reduce the sauce until it thickens. Plate and sprinkle with white sesame seeds, then start to enjoy.
