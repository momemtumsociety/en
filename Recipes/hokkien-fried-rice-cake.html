Hokkien Fried Rice Cake

Ingredients

Sliced BAIGUO (Rice Cake) 450g

Sliced pork 25g

Chopped garlic 5g

Dried shredded squid 10g

Sliced fish cake Few pieces

Shelled shrimp 3-5 units

Cut celery 25g

Sauce and Others

Light soy sauce 1.5 tablespoon

White pepper powder 1/2 teaspoon

Tickening starch 10g

Oyster sauce 1/2 tablespoon

Black soy sauce 1 tablespoons

Flounder fish powder 1/2 tablespoon

Instructions

  1. Marinate the pork with light soy sauce, white pepper powder and thickening starch for 10 minutes.
  2. Sauté minced garlic and shredded squid in lard, then add pork, fish cake and shrimp in turn until half cooked.
  3. Then, add BAIGUO, oyster sauce, light soy sauce, black soy sauce, white pepper powder and flounder fish powder and stir-fry well.
  4. Add a little water and simmer for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the celery and stir-fry, then simmer for a while. When the celery is cooked, you can serve it on a plate.
