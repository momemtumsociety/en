Crispy Roasted Rice Cake

Ingredients

BAIGUO (Rice Cake) 450g

Sauce and Others

Tomato sauce 2 tablespoons

Sugar 1 teaspoon

Barbecue powder 2 teaspoons

Chili powder 1 teaspoon

Light soy sauce 0.5 tablespoon

Water 1 tablespoon

Instructions

  1. Mix all the "Sauce and Others" well.
  2. Pour a little cooking oil into a non-stick pan and fry the BAIGUO on both sides over low heat until lightly browned. (You can fry them longer if you like a crispy texture.)
  3. Pour in the sauce and reduce over high heat until thickened, then enjoy.
