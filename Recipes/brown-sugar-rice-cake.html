Brown Sugar Rice Cake

Ingredients

Sliced BAIGUO (Rice Cake) 360g

Brown sugar 70g

White sesame 20g

Sauce and Others

Hot water 150g

Instructions

  1. Melt the brown sugar in boiling water.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the BAIGUO until golden brown on both sides.
  3. Then pour in the brown sugar water, turn down the heat and reduce the sauce until it thickens. Plate and sprinkle with white sesame seeds, then start to enjoy.
