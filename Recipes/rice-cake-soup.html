Rice Cake Soup

Ingredients

Sliced BAIGUO (Rice Cake) 450g

Sliced lean pork 35g

Sliced fatty pork 25g

Whisked egg 3 units

Sliced mushrooms 25g

Bok choy 30g

Sauce and Others

Salt 1 teaspoon

FUZHOU red wine 2 tablespoon

Oyster sauce 1 tablespoon

Chicken essence powder / MSG 1/2 teaspoon

Hot water

Instructions

  1. Marinate the pork lean meat with salt for 10 minutes. While waiting, scramble the eggs and set aside.
  2. Heat the wok, add the fatty pork to squeeze out the oil, then add the lean pork and mushrooms and stir-fry.
  3. Then, Add the scrambled eggs, FUZHOU red wine, oyster sauce, salt and chicken essence powder and stir-fry.
  4. Add the BAIGUO, and then add hot water to cover the ingredients and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Lastly, add the bok choy and cook for another one minute. When the bok choy is cooked, you can serve it in a bowl.
