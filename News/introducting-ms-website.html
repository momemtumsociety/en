Introducing MS Website

Momentum Society (MS) today launched the MS webpage with the aim of enabling customers or companies to get information about the products and learn more about MS at anytime and anywhere, and thus become interested in purchasing MS products.

Product information

MS webpage provides basic information about the product, which includes the size and weight of the product, the ingredients, the method of storage and shelf life.
The simple ingredients and zero preservative additions result in a relatively short shelf life for most of MS products. Customers can consume with a peace of mind.

Provide multiple ways to consume the products

With the MS recipes provided on the MS website, there are many ways for customers to serve MS products. Instead of being limited to the traditional way of cooking, MS will keep collecting, developing and updating new ways of serving and updating the webpage.
The large number of recipes with different flavours will help MS to expand customer base, and the customers will have new taste experiences.
In addition, MS will try and validate the recipes after publishing. Recipes that pass the validation process will display an orange flag to indicate that the recipe is worth trying. MS also welcome suggestions from the customers on the recipes, and a critical review will be taken off before any necessary update.
