Delivery Area

MS only offer the Store Delivery service in SIBU (Sibu 96000, Sarawak, Malaysia) area for now.

Delivery Charge

A delivery charge of RM5 will be added to the total amount if the total amount purchased by the customer is less than RM25. For the customer purchased over or equal to RM25, MS won't cost he/she anything.

Penalty Order

Twelve hours prior to 12:00 a.m. on the due date, we will discontinue the service for customers to make changes to their orders. Any changes made to the products during this period will force the order to be classified as a "Penalty Order". In addition, any order placed less than 24 hours before the due date will also be classified as a "Penalty Order".

A delivery charge of RM10 will be levied on the customer for the delivery service provided for "Penalty Orders" and the delivery reduction will not apply to any "Penalty Orders".

Order will be delivered on

Monday to Friday, 9am to 4pm. MS are off on public holiday.

MS will send a purchase order for the customer after he/she places the order. A message will be sent to inform the customer before the order arrives at your doorstep.

What happens if the order delivered is damaged?

In case of any problems with your delivery, MS wil be in help to resolve the issue as quickly as possible with as little inconvenience to you. MS will make every effort to contact you and keep you informed of the progress of out investigation and where appropriate, deliver a replacement. If you have already received your order and find that it is damaged, please contact MS.
